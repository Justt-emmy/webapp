About Company


Founded in the year 2024 by Ajibo Chukwuemeka Sunday, popularly known as Justt Emmy.
Justt Tech was established with a clear mission: to redefine the digital landscape through innovative website development and comprehensive technology insights. Headquartered in Lagos, Nigeria our team of skilled professionals works tirelessly to bring tech ideas to life, empowering businesses and inspiring readers in the fast-paced world of technology. Ajibo Chukwuemeka is a seasoned professional with a passion for technology and a background in Web Development and Application. With a zeal for combining technical skills and creative vision, he set out to build a company that values both quality and impact. Driven by a desire to make a difference in the tech world, he continues to lead Justt Tech towards innovation and growth.
